# Millie's techNL Bootcamp-Portfolio
A collection of skills I gained and projects I created during the techNL Bootcamp, Summer of 2026.

## Overview
Throughout the 2 weeks of Bootcamp, I explored an array of technical skills, soft skills and platforms as well as courses and panels, gaining experience and exposure in the Technology industry. I learnt a lot and broadened my portfolio of digital and interpersonnal skills.

### Project
The biggest project I worked on in Bootcamp was a Group Game. We were put into teams of 5 and tasked to make a game in two weeks. The parameters included that it had to be a one level maze game that was beatable in 5 minutes.

I was one of our major group coders and our game designer. 

My contributions included:
1. Coding and designing booby traps
2. Coding and designing an Enemy to fight against
3. Designing our large game play map
4. Combining our group code into one functionning game
5. Coding health bars and shadow features

[Play Shadow Maze!](https://makecode.com/_55VFAF0m9hzd)
